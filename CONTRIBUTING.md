# Contributing to Awesome German YouTube Channels

Thank you for your interest in contributing to this awesome list! We welcome contributions from the community.

## How to Contribute

### Adding a Channel

1. **Fork this repository** to your own GitHub account
2. **Clone your fork** to your local machine
3. **Create a new branch** for your changes
4. **Add the channel** to the appropriate category in all language versions:
   - `README.md` (English)
   - `README.de.md` (German)
   - `README.ja.md` (Japanese)
5. **Ensure the channel meets the criteria** (see below)
6. **Commit your changes** with a clear commit message
7. **Push to your fork** and submit a pull request

### Channel Criteria

To be included in this list, a YouTube channel should:

- Focus on teaching German language or German culture
- Provide value to German learners at any level
- Have quality content with regular updates
- Be freely accessible on YouTube
- Have a reasonable number of subscribers and engagement

### Formatting Guidelines

When adding a channel, please follow this format:

```markdown
- [Channel Name](https://www.youtube.com/c/ChannelURL) - Brief description of what the channel offers.
```

- Keep descriptions concise (one sentence)
- Use consistent formatting with existing entries
- Place the channel in the most appropriate category
- Maintain alphabetical order within categories where possible

### Multi-language Support

All changes must be reflected in all three language versions:

- **English** (README.md): Use English descriptions
- **German** (README.de.md): Translate descriptions to German
- **Japanese** (README.ja.md): Translate descriptions to Japanese

### What NOT to Contribute

- Channels with offensive or inappropriate content
- Channels that are no longer active or have been deleted
- Duplicate entries already in the list
- Channels not related to German language learning
- Paid or subscription-only content (unless clearly marked)

## Questions?

If you have questions about contributing, feel free to open an issue for discussion.

## Code of Conduct

Please be respectful and constructive in all interactions. We aim to maintain a welcoming environment for all contributors.
